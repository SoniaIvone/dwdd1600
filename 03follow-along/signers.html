Who Were the Signers

Famour Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
The principal author of the Declaration and later the third President of the United States.
John Adams
A prominent advocate for independence who later became the second President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned inventor, statesman, and diplomat, Franklin was instrumental in gaining French support for the American Revolution.
John Hancock
Famous for his bold and prominent signature, he was the President of the Continental Congress at the time of signing.
Samuel Adams
A key organizer of the Boston Tea Party and a passionate advocate for colonial independence.

These individuals played significant roles not only in drafting and signing the Declaration but also in shaping the early United States.
