About Us

Who We Are

We are Kalobe and Sonia Monroy-Selu, the founders of Socials by Selu. We are dreamers with a vision—everything we do is for our children and to build the life we’ve always dreamed of for them. Our goal is simple: to provide the best life possible for our family while spending every precious moment together. That’s why we’ve started this business. We believe in working hard to earn back our time and live life on our own terms, sharing our journey with those who value freedom, family, and growth.

Why We Started Socials by Selu

We created Socials by Selu because we wanted to change the way we live and work. We have an entrepreneurial mindset that drives us to build a business around our lifestyle, giving us the flexibility to be there for our kids and loved ones while doing meaningful work. By combining Sonia’s business expertise and Kalobe’s sales background, we’re passionate about helping businesses grow through creative, tailored social media strategies that produce real results.
